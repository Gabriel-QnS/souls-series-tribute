Dark Souls Series

The Dark Souls franchise is a series of action role-playing games developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It began with the release of Dark Souls (2011) and has seen two sequels, Dark Souls II (2014) and Dark Souls III (2016).

The games take place within a dark, medieval fantasy setting, where the player’s character fights against knights, dragons, phantoms, demons, and other monstrous or supernatural entities.

Hidetaka Miyazaki, the director of the Dark Souls franchise, has stated that he is a huge masochist. The series is known for its high level of difficulty, which is one of its most discussed aspects.

The challenge of overcoming adversity and the sense of accomplishment that comes with it is what makes the game appealing to many players.

You can access specific information about the games, characters, items and story in one of the many Wikis created and maintained by the community: Dark Souls Wiki
